01 ISO 9001:2015 Standard

Relationship between PDCA, documentation, and QMS standard requirements.

02 Auditing Concepts

Principles, methods, and ISO 19011 auditing guidelines for all audit types.

03 Roles & Competency

Auditor responsibilities, qualification criteria, and professional ethics.

04 Planning an Audit

Pre-audit planning, document review, and checklist preparation.

05 Execution & Reporting

Opening/closing meetings, evidence collection, and nonconformity reporting.
