What will you learn?

Our comprehensive QMS Lead Auditor Course offers an enriching online learning experience to help you enrich your proficiency and expertise required to conduct a Quality Management System (QMS) audit for an organization adhering to ISO 9001:2015 standard.

Throughout this ISO 9001:2015 lead auditor course, we will delve into the intricacies of ISO 9001:2015, equipping you with the knowledge required to navigate the world of quality management systems effectively. You will not only grasp the core concepts and requirements of the standard but also learn the latest techniques in auditing methodologies.

Learning Outcomes

At the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Gain essential knowledge and understanding of QMS ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 19011:2018 audit process.
  • Assess the QMS of an organization in accordance with ISO 9001:2015 requirements to ensure improved performance.
  • Plan, Conduct, Manage, and Complete the QMS audit in accordance with ISO 19011 principles.
  • Understand document requirements by referring to sample organization documents.

Course Outline

A comprehensive 5-step curriculum covering standard requirements to audit execution.

01 QMS Concepts & ISO 9001:2015

  • • QMS concepts and benefits
  • • Relationship between PDCA and ISO 9001:2015
  • • ISO 9001:2015 standard requirements
  • • QMS documentation

02 Auditing Concepts

  • • Audit objectives and auditing principles
  • • Types of audits (1st, 2nd, and 3rd party)
  • • Audit methods and the systematic process
  • • ISO 19011 auditing guidelines

03 Roles & Competency

  • • Auditor and lead auditor's responsibilities
  • • Competency and qualification of auditors
  • • Auditor evaluation methods
  • • Professional conduct and ethics

04 Planning an Audit

  • • Pre-audit planning and document review
  • • Developing a detailed audit plan
  • • Preparing checklists and working documents
  • • Essential communication factors

05 Conducting an Audit & Nonconformities

  • • Opening meeting protocols
  • • Effective interviewing techniques
  • • Collecting and verifying objective evidence
  • • Identifying and recording nonconformities
  • • Closing meeting and reporting
  • • Do's and Don'ts of professional auditing
